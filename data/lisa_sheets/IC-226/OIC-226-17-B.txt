Before stopping the anticoagulant treatment prescribed for PE, it is important to ensure that there is no [[Dyspnoea SD-162|dyspnoea]]. In the event of dyspnoea, it is essential to detect [[https://sides.uness.fr/lisa/Savoir_%C3%A9voquer_les_complications_%C3%A0_long_terme_de_la_MTEV_(post-thrombotic_syndrome,_HTAP)_2C-226-SP-B01 post-embolic pulmonary hypertension (PH)] by first performing a transthoracic Doppler cardiac ultrasound.
