==I) Obliterative arteriopathy of the lower limbs===

*[[File:Atheroma.jpg|vignette]]'''Definition:'''  Obliterative arterial disease of the lower limbs (AOMI) is defined as the '''presence of atherosclerotic lesions in the aorta and underlying arteries perfusing the lower limbs''. It can lead to plaques (atheromatous plaque), stenosis or atheromatous obstructions of one or more arteries serving the pelvis and lower limbs.

*'''Frequency:'''' The prevalence of the disease increases with age, reaching more than '''20% over the age of 70.  '''
